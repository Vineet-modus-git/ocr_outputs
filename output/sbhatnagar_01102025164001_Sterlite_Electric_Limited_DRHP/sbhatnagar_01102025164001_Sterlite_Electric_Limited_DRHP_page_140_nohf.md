Sterlite Electric Limited Restricted Stock Unit Scheme 2025 ("RSU 2025")

RSU 2025, which is an employee stock option scheme, was instituted pursuant to a resolution dated September 2, 2025, of our Board and resolution dated September 26, 2025, of our Shareholders.

The maximum number of RSUs which can be granted under RSU 2025 is 1,257,497 RSUs.

As on the date of this Draft Red Herring Prospectus, no RSUs are granted under RSU 2025.
