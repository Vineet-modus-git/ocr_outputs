## Shareholders' agreements

Shareholders' agreement dated November 8, 2024, read with the Deed of Adherence dated June 16, 2025, entered into amongst our Company, Twin Star Overseas Limited, South Asia Growth Fund III Holdings, LLC ("GEF-I"), South Asia EBT Trust III ("GEF-II", collectively with GEF-I, referred to as "GEF"), Volrado Venture Partners Fund III – BETA ("Volrado"), Protons Corporate Services Private Limited ("Protons") and Trufort Fund Limited ("Trufort, collectively, with GEF, Volrado and Protons referred to as "Investors" and collectively with our Company and Twin Star Overseas Limited as "Parties") ("Shareholders' Agreement"), as amended by the waiver cum amendment agreement dated September 29, 2025, ("Waiver cum Amendment Agreement")

Our Company, Twin Star Overseas Limited, GEF, Volrado, Protons and Trufort have entered into the Shareholders' Agreement to record their understanding as to the operation, management and governance of our Company and its Subsidiaries, their inter- se rights and obligations as shareholders, the terms of management and governance of our Company and the matters incidental and ancillary thereto.

In terms of the Shareholders' Agreement, the Parties have certain rights and obligations, amongst others:

A. **Board appointment:** GEF is entitled to appoint one director on our Board, subject to them holding more than 4% of the total share capital of our Company on a fully diluted basis. GEF is also entitled to nominate any person to act as an alternate director to their nominee director.

B. **Observer:** GEF and Volrado are entitled to nominate one observer each on our Board and the Risk Management and ESG Committee and IPO Committee ("Identified Committees"), subject to them holding more than 2% of the total share capital of our Company on a fully diluted basis.

C. **Committee nomination:** GEF is entitled to appoint their nominee director as member on the Identified Committees and Volrado is entitled to nominate a representative as member of the IPO Committee, subject to them holding more than 4% of the total share capital of our Company on a fully diluted basis.

D. **Reserved matters:** Our Company is required to obtain consent of GEF and Volrado for undertaking the certain actions, including the following in connection with the Offer:

(a) changes in the charter documents of our Company;

(b) any transfer of or encumbrance over the securities held by Twin Star Overseas Limited where such securities either through a single transaction or through a combination of multiple transactions represent more than 10% of the total share capital of our Company on a fully diluted basis or results in the holding of Twin Star Overseas Limited and its affiliates being less than 51% of the total share capital of our Company on a fully diluted basis ("Substantial Sale");

(c) any re-purchase, buyback, redemption or other cancellation of any equity or preference share capital of our Company;

(d) unless otherwise provided for in the annual budget, capital expenditures incurred by the Company and/or its Subsidiaries (including constructions and leases) exceeding an aggregate of ₹ 500.00 million; and

(e) issuance of, adoption of, or alteration to terms / quantum of existing ESOP plans or any new employee stock incentive places.

E. **Pre-emptive rights:** If our Company issues or proposes to issue any new securities including by way of a rights issue or preferential allotment (except for issuance of Equity Shares by way of the Offer, issuance of Equity Shares pursuant to exercise of options or conversion of convertible instruments), our Company is required to provide a pre-emptive right to Twin Star Overseas Limited, and Investors to subscribe to their respective pro-rata portion of such new issuance of securities, subject to them holding at least 2% of the total share capital of our Company on a fully diluted basis.
