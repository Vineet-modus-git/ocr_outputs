### Principal terms of the borrowings availed by our Company and its Subsidiaries:

The details provided below are indicative and there may be additional terms, conditions and requirements under the various financing documentation executed by our Company and Subsidiaries in relation to our indebtedness.

* **Tenor and repayment:** The tenor of the term loans availed by us typically range from 12 months to 60 months. The tenor of the working capital facilities availed by us typically range from 1 month to 12 months, and are subject to renewal on basis, a fixed time frame or at the discretion of the respective lenders. Certain of our working capital facilities are repayable on demand.

* **Interest rate:** The applicable rate of interest for the various facilities availed by us are typically linked to benchmark rates, such as the long-term lending rate and marginal cost of lending rate, external benchmark lending rate, repo rate of a specified lender over a specific period of time plus a specified spread per annum and are subject to mutual discussions between the relevant lenders and our Company and Subsidiaries. The rate of interest for the term loan facilities availed by us typically ranges from 7% per annum to 10% per annum. The working capital facilities, which includes cash credit, invoice discounting, overdraft facility and import letter of credit have a floating rate of interest, at mutually agreed rates.

* **Security:** In terms of our borrowing arrangements, we are typically required to create security primarily by way of first ranking pari passu charge, by way of hypothecation on current assets and movable fixed assets of our Company.

* **Pre-payment:** Our borrowings typically have pre-payment provisions which allow for pre-payment of the outstanding amount at any given point in time, subject to the conditions specified in the borrowing arrangements. Certain of the borrowings availed by us carry a pre-payment penalty which ranges up to 2% to such higher rate on the pre-paid amount based on lenders extant guidelines or as may be at the discretion of respective lenders.
