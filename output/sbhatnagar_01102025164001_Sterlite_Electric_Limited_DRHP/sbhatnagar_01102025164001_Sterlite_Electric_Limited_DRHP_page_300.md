# Key Managerial Personnel and Senior Management

## Key Managerial Personnel

In addition to Pratik Pravin Agarwal, the Managing Director of our Company, and Reshu Madan, the Whole-time Director and Chief Executive Officer of our Company, whose details are provided in “- Brief profiles of our Directors” on page 265, the details of our other Key Managerial Personnel in terms of the SEBI ICDR Regulations, as of the date of this Draft Red Herring Prospectus are set forth below:

**Monica Madan** is the Chief Financial Officer of our Company. She is responsible for overseeing financial strategy, planning, and risk management to optimize business performance. She holds a bachelor's degree in commerce from G.G.D.S.D College, Chandigarh, Panjab University. She is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. She has experience in the field of accounting, financial, commercial and taxation related matters. Prior to joining our Company, she was associated with Schneider Electric Ireland Limited, Shell India Markets Private Limited, Microsoft India (R&D) Private Limited. She is associated with our Company since December 5, 2024. In Fiscal 2025, she received a remuneration of ₹ 1.51 million from our Company.

**Ashok Ganesan** is the Company Secretary and Compliance Officer of our Company. He is responsible for ensuring corporate governance, regulatory compliance, and board-level secretarial functions. He holds a bachelor's degree in law from Lloyd's Law College and a master's degree in commerce from the Himachal Pradesh University. He is a fellow member of Institute of Company Secretaries of India. He has 25 years of experience in legal and compliance sector. Prior to joining our Company, he was associated with DLF Building India, IREO Management Advisors India Private Limited, Moserbaer Projects Private Limited, Hindustan Power Projects Private Limited and Hi-Tech Gears Limited. He is associated with our Company since April 10, 2017. In Fiscal 2025, he received a remuneration of ₹16.28 million from our Company.
