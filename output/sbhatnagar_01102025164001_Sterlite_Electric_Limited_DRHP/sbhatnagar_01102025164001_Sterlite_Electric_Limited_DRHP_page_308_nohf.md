In accordance with the SEBI ICDR Regulations, for the purpose of identification and disclosure of group companies in this Draft Red Herring Prospectus, our Company has formulated a policy for identification of group companies and considered (i) the companies (other than our Corporate Promoter and Subsidiaries) with which there were related party transactions, as disclosed in the Restated Consolidated Financial Information as per Ind AS 24 'Related Party Disclosures'; and (ii) any other companies considered material by the board of directors of our Company. With respect to point (ii), pursuant to Materiality Policy, the companies (other than our Corporate Promoter and our Subsidiaries and the companies categorized under (i) above) that are forming part of the Promoter Group (in terms of Regulation 2(1)(pp) of the SEBI ICDR Regulations) with which there were one or more transactions for the financial year ended March 31, 2025, ("Test Period") which individually or in the aggregate, exceed 10% of the restated consolidated revenue from operations, as derived from the Restated Consolidated Financial Information for the respective Test Period, shall be considered as Group Companies.
