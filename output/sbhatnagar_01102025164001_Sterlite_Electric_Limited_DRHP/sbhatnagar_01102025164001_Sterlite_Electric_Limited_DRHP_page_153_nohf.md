(1) Revenue from operations refers to the revenue from continuing operations.

(2) Others comprises of revenue from IRU/ARC contracts, revenue from network infrastructure, revenue from management fees and sale of scrap.

(3) EBITDA is computed as profit for the year from continuing operations plus total tax expense (continuing operations), finance costs (continuing operations), depreciation and amortisation expense (continuing operations).

(4) EBITDA margin % is computed as EBITDA divided by Total income (continuing operations).

(5) PAT refers to profit for the year from continuing operations

(6) PAT margin % is computed as PAT divided by Total income (continuing operations).

(7) Return on Equity % is calculated as PAT divided by Average Equity. Average Equity is calculated as average of Total equity at the beginning and at the end of the relevant fiscal.

(8) Return on Capital Employed % is calculated as EBIT divided by Capital employed. EBIT is computed as EBITDA less depreciation and amortisation expense (continuing operations). Capital employed is calculated as Tangible Net worth plus Total Borrowings plus Deferred tax liabilities (net). Tangible Net worth is calculated as total equity less intangible assets (including intangible assets under development) less Right-of-use assets.

(9) Operating Working Capital is calculated as inventories plus trade receivables less trade payables and acceptances.

(10) Equity means total equity.

(11) Fixed Assets represents gross block of property, plant and equipment and right-of-use assets as at the end of the relevant fiscal.

(12) Total borrowings refers to aggregate of non-current borrowings and current borrowings.

(13) Net debt/(Net Cash Surplus) is calculated as Total borrowings, less cash and cash equivalents and other current bank balances.

(14) Fixed Asset Turnover Ratio is calculated as Revenue from Operations divided by average Fixed Assets. Average Fixed Assets is calculated as average of Fixed Assets at the beginning and at the end of the relevant fiscal.

(15) Debt to Equity Ratio is calculated as Total borrowings divided by Equity.

(16) Net Debt/(Net Cash Surplus) to EBITDA is calculated as Net Debt/(Net Cash Surplus) divided by EBITDA.

(17) Closing order book = The amount of order book is calculated as the total contract value (as per the terms of the contract / attendant documents / addendums) of all existing contracts, minus any revenue already recognised by the Company in relation to such existing contracts.

(18) Capacity = Capacity indicates the production capability for cables (km), OPGW (km) and conductors (MT - equivalent to Al59 Product)

(19) Financial information as at March 31, 2023 is not comparable with the corresponding information as at March 31, 2024 and March 31, 2025.
