<table><thead><tr><td>Term</td><td>Description</td></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>EBITDA</td><td>EBITDA is computed as profit for the year from continuing operations plus total tax expense (continuing operations), finance costs (continuing operations), depreciation and amortisation expense (continuing operations)</td></tr><tr><td>EBITDA Margin %</td><td>EBITDA margin % is computed as EBITDA divided by Total income (continuing operations)</td></tr><tr><td>Equity</td><td>Equity means total equity</td></tr><tr><td>Fixed Asset Turnover Ratio</td><td>Fixed Asset Turnover Ratio is calculated as Revenue from Operations divided by average Fixed Assets. EBIT is computed as EBITDA less depreciation and amortisation expense (continuing operations). Capital employed is calculated as Tangible Net worth plus Total Borrowings plus Deferred tax liabilities (net). Tangible Net worth is calculated as total equity less intangible assets (including intangible assets under development) less Right-of-use assets.</td></tr><tr><td>Return on Equity%</td><td>Return on Equity% is calculated as PAT divided by Average Equity. Average Equity is calculated as average of Total equity at the beginning and at the end of the relevant fiscal</td></tr><tr><td>Revenue from operations</td><td>Revenue from operations refers to the revenue from continuing operations.</td></tr><tr><td>Total Borrowings</td><td>Total Borrowings refers to aggregate of non-current borrowings and current borrowings</td></tr></tbody></table>
