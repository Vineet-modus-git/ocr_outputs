# SECTION VII – PARTICULARS OF THE ISSUE

## OBJECTS OF THE ISSUE

This Issue includes a fresh Issue of upto 70,00,000 Equity Shares of our Company at an Issue Price of ₹ [●] per Equity Share. The proceeds of the Issue, after deducting the Issue related expenses, are estimated to be ₹ [●] lakhs ("Net Proceeds"). We intend to utilize the Net Proceeds of the Issue to meet the following objects:

1. Funding capital expenditure requirements towards funding of new equipment and machinery;

2. Repayment/pre-payment, in full or in part, of certain borrowings availed by our Company; and

3. General Corporate Purposes.

*(Collectively referred as the "Objects")*

The main objects clause and objects incidental and ancillary to the main objects as set out in the Memorandum of Association enable our Company to undertake our existing business activities and to undertake the activities proposed for which the funds are being raised in the Issue.
