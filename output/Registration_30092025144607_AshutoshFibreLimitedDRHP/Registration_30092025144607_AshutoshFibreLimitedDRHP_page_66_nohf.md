SECTION V - GENERAL INFORMATION

Our Company was originally incorporated on May 21, 1985 under the name "Ashutosh Fibre Private Limited" at Ahmedabad, Gujarat, under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 and the Certificate of Incorporation was issued at Ahmedabad by Registrar of Companies, Gujarat bearing Registration Number 007831 of 1985. The present CIN of our Company is U24299GJ1985PLC007831.
