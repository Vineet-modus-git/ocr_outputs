## OUR COMPETITIVE STRENGTH

### Recycling and Sustainable Fibre Solutions

We have developed capabilities in recycling and re-engineering para-aramid fabrics, which are difficult to process due to their high tensile strength, thermal resistance and non-biodegradable nature. Para-aramid yarns, widely used in safety and protective applications such as bulletproof vests, are imported by us at the end of their usable life. We process these expired materials through our proprietary recycling system and convert them into high-quality secondary raw materials.
