B Restated Statement of Employee Benefits- Gratuity

The Company operates a defined benefit gratuity plan through a trust. Under the plan, every employee is entitled to a benefit equivalent to fifteen days' salary last drawn for each completed year of service. The benefit is payable on termination of employment, retirement, or death, whichever is earlier, and vests after five years of continuous service.
