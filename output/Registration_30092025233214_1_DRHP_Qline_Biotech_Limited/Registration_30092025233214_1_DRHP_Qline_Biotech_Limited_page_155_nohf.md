Q-Line Iris Private Limited is authorized to operate and manage radiology and imaging labs, screening centers, research and scan centers, and related infrastructure both in India and abroad. The company may procure, install, and maintain radiological and imaging equipment, provide skilled medical manpower (like radiologists and technicians), and offer training to medical staff and students. Additionally, it can set up and operate centers for medical investigations and tests (e.g., CT, MRI, ECG, nuclear scans) across various healthcare models (turnkey, PPP, etc.), and act as a service provider to public and private healthcare institutions. It is also authorized to handle the installation, servicing, and maintenance of radiology equipment and build the necessary infrastructure to support its services as authorized under its memorandum of association.
