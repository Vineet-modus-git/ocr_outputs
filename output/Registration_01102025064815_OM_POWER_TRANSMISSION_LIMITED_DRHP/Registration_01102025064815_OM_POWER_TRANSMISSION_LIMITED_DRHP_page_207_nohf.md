## EPC Segment

### Overview: EPC services & advantages

Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) is a widely adopted project delivery model in the power infrastructure sector, where the contractor assumes full responsibility for the project from design to commissioning. EPC contracts streamline execution by combining all critical activities under a single entity, ensuring accountability and efficiency. This integrated approach reduces the complexities of multi-vendor coordination, minimizes delays, and provides a predictable cost and time framework for project owners.
