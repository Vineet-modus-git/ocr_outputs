**Notes:**

1. Total Income means addition of revenue from operations and other income.

2. Revenue from Operations means addition of revenue from customers and other operating income.

3. PAT means profit for the year provides information regarding the overall profitability of the business.

4. Net Operating Cash Flows means Cash Generated from Operations after income taxes paid.

5. PAT Margin (%) = PAT / Revenue from Operations.

6. EBITDA = Restated profit after tax for the year before exceptional items + finance costs + total tax expense/(credit) + depreciation and amortisation expense excluding other income.

7. EBITDA Margin (%) = EBITDA / Revenue from Operations.

8. Return on Equity is calculated as restated profit after tax for the year divided by average total equity.

9. Return on Capital Employed (%) is calculated as earning before interest and tax (EBIT) / Capital Employed. EBIT is calculated as "Profit before tax + Interest expenses" and Capital Employed is calculated as "Total Equity + Non-Current Borrowings + Current Borrowing + Deferred Tax Asset/(Liability) - Intangible Assets including Intangible Assets under Development".

10. CFO/EBITDA is calculated as net operating cash flows from operations divided by EBITDA.

11. Debt to Equity ratio is calculated as Total of "non-current borrowings and current borrowings" / Total Equity.

12. Current ratio is calculated as restated total current assets divided by total current liabilities.

13. Order Book is calculated as estimated contract value of the unexecuted portion of a company's existing assigned contracts.

14. Number of projects completed means projects completed in the respective period.

15. Number of customers served means customers for the respective year. Such number of customers may consist of common parties in all of the respective year.

16. Bill to Book ratio is calculated as Order Book as at a particular period divided by the Revenue from Operations for that period.

17. Order inflow means the amount of orders won for a particular period.

For any further details of our KPIs, see "Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Position and Results of Operations - Key Performance Indicators" on page 358.
