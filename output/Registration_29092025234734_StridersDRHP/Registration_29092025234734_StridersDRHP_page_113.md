Our Company proposes to utilize the Net Proceeds from the Fresh Offer towards funding the following objects:

1. Funding of working capital requirements of the Company;

2. Investment in Striders FZ LLC, wholly owned subsidiary, to fund its working capital requirements;

3. Incorporation and Investment in a newly proposed wholly owned subsidiary in mainland UAE and funding its working capital requirements;

4. Repayment of Loans;

5. General corporate purposes;
