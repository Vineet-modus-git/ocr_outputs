## FRESH ISSUE

The Issue Proceeds from the Fresh Issue will be utilized towards the following objects:

1. Funding the capital expenditure requirements of our company by investment in Purchase of Vehicles, Modification, Development & Approval Cost.

2. Funding the working capital requirements of our Company, and

3. Loan to subsidiary Company, Ecofuel Cylinders Private Limited to meet its Working Capital requirement.

4. General corporate purposes.
