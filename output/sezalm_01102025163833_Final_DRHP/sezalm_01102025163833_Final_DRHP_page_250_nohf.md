## Q. Other Expenses

Other expenses increased by **106.53%** to **₹497.79 lakhs in Fiscal 2024** from **₹241.03 lakhs in Fiscal 2023**, primarily due to a significant rise in homologation and certification costs for BS VI CNG conversion kits. Homologation expenses rose to **₹155.87 lakhs in Fiscal 2024** from **₹32.01 lakhs in Fiscal 2023**, representing **31.31%** of total other expenses in Fiscal 2024 as compared to **13.28%** in the previous year.
